Selection Process Explained

The selection process for ONGC Apprentice Recruitment 2025 is simple and entirely merit-based. There is no written exam, making it an excellent opportunity for candidates with strong academic performance.

Merit-Based Selection

Candidates will be selected based on the marks obtained in their qualifying examinations (ITI, Diploma, or Graduate Degree). A merit list will be prepared for each trade and work centre according to these scores. In the event of a tie, preference will be given to the older candidate.
