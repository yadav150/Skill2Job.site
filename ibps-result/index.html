IBPS Result 2025

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has announced the Preliminary Examination results for the recruitment of Probationary Officers (PO) and Management Trainees (MT) under the Common Recruitment Process (CRP PO/MT-XV). Candidates who appeared in the online prelims can now check their results and download their scorecards from the official IBPS portal. The examination was conducted on 17, 23, and 24 August 2025.

Applicants who qualify in the preliminary exam are eligible to appear for the Main Examination. This article provides complete details along with direct links to access the IBPS PO Result 2025.

How to Check PO/MT Result

Candidates who appeared in the CRP PO/MT-XV Preliminary Examination can check their results through the official IBPS website. To access the result, candidates must log in using their Registration Number and Password or Date of Birth. Once logged in, they can view their result status and download the scorecard for future use.
