NMMS Exam 2025

The Directorate of Secondary Education, Assam has released the official notification for the National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS) Examination 2025. Students of Class VIII are invited to apply online for the examination scheduled on 15th November 2025 across Assam. The scheme is sponsored by the Department of School Education & Literacy, Ministry of Education, Government of India. Interested candidates can submit their applications through the official portal https://bidyarthi.co.in or via the Bidyarthi Mobile App.

NMMS Scholarship Exam Eligibility

Class: Students pursuing studies in Class VIII are eligible to appear for the NMMS Examination.

How To Apply for NMMS Exam 2025

Eligible students who wish to appear for the NMMS Examination 2025 must submit their applications via the official web portal or the DSE, Assam mobile app. The online application process can be completed by following these steps:

Scroll down and locate the Important Links section on the website.
