Grade III Driver (HSLC)

Exam Overview: The ADRE Driver (HSLC) Exam is conducted offline in a written format. It lasts 3 hours (180 minutes) and consists of 150 multiple-choice questions, totaling 150 marks. Each incorrect answer carries a negative marking of one-fourth of the allotted marks.

Subjects: The exam covers Social Studies, General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning and Mental Ability, General English, General Mathematics, and Road Transport.

ADRE Grade-IV Syllabus

This section provides the complete syllabus for the Assam Direct Recruitment (ADRE) Grade-IV Examination. Like the Grade-III exams, the Grade-IV exams are organized for two educational categories: Class VIII and Class X (Matriculation), with exams tailored to each level.

Grade IV Class VIII

Exam Overview: The ADRE Class VIII Exam is a written offline test of 2 hours 30 minutes, consisting of 135 multiple-choice questions totaling 135 marks. Each incorrect answer carries a negative marking of 0.25 marks.

Subjects: The exam includes English, Social Studies, Mathematics, General Knowledge, and Mental Ability & Logical Reasoning. Candidates should focus on each subject according to its weightage to maximize scores.

Grade IV Class X (Matriculation)

Exam Overview: The ADRE Class X (Matriculation) Exam is a written offline test of 2 hours 30 minutes, comprising 135 multiple-choice questions with a total of 135 marks. Each wrong answer carries a negative marking of 0.25 marks.

Subjects: The exam covers English, Social Studies, Mathematics, General Knowledge, and Mental Ability & Logical Reasoning. Candidates should prepare each subject carefully according to its weightage to achieve maximum marks.

FAQs - Assam Direct Recruitment Syllabus

1. What is the ADRE exam syllabus for Grade-III?

The Grade-III syllabus includes Social Studies, General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning, Mental Ability, General English, General Mathematics, and Road Transport, with specific marks and MCQs for each subject.

2. How many questions are in the ADRE HSSLC exam?

The HSSLC exam consists of 150 multiple-choice questions totaling 150 marks, covering Social Studies, General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning, General English, and General Mathematics.

3. Is there negative marking in ADRE exams?

Yes, all ADRE exams have negative marking. Each incorrect answer carries one-fourth (0.25) of the marks allotted for that question, applicable to all categories.

4. What subjects are included in the ADRE Bachelor Degree exam?

The Bachelor Degree exam includes Social Studies, General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning, General Mathematics, General English, and Reading Comprehension & English Language with specified marks.

5. Where can I download the ADRE syllabus?

The ADRE syllabus can be downloaded from official SEBA links for Grade-III and Grade-IV, as well as from the official website, WhatsApp channel, and Facebook page.
