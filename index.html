About Us

Skill2Job provides the latest Assam government jobs, exam results, admissions, and scholarship updates. Stay updated with reliable information for job seekers and students in Assam and Northeast India.

Our mission is to deliver timely and accurate updates that guide aspirants towards better opportunities. From job vacancies to educational admissions and scholarships, Skill2Job is designed to simplify access to important information, helping the youth of Assam and the Northeast take confident steps toward their career and academic goals.

Skill2Job is solely founded, managed, and developed by Yadav Subba. His vision is to empower students and job seekers with a trusted platform that provides clear, authentic, and useful updates. Through dedication and hard work, he continues to grow Skill2Job as a reliable resource for thousands of aspirants across the region.
