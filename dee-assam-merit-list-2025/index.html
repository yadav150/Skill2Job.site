DEE Assam 2nd Merit List 2025

The Directorate of Elementary Education (DEE), Assam has officially released the DEE Assam 2nd Provisional Merit List 2025 for the recruitment of Assistant Teachers in Lower Primary (LP) and Upper Primary (UP) schools. This updated merit list has been published in reference to Advertisement Nos. E-383723/117 and E-383723/124, both dated 26 December 2023. Candidates who applied for the DEE Assam LP UP Teacher Recruitment 2025 can now check and download the merit list PDF from the official website of DEE, Assam.

The DEE Assam 2nd Merit List was made available on 25 October 2025. Along with the list, the Directorate has also shared the schedule for candidates to submit any genuine grievances. The DEE Assam Final Merit List 2025 will be published later, and only those candidates whose names appear in it will receive appointment letters. The distribution of appointment letters for LP and UP teacher vacancies is expected to take place in November 2025.

Key Information About DEE Assam Merit List 2025

The DEE Assam 2nd Provisional Merit List 2025 for LP & UP Teacher Recruitment has been prepared based on the details and documents submitted by candidates during the online application and document verification process. While seeing your name here is exciting, it does not guarantee an appointment. Final appointment letters will only be issued after the Final Select List is released.
