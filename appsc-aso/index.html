APPSC ASO Recruitment 2025

The Arunachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC), Itanagar has officially released a new recruitment notification for 2025. The announcement is available on its website appsc.gov.in, inviting applications for the post of Assistant Section Officer (ASO) through Limited Departmental Competitive Examination.

According to the notification, the APPSC is conducting this recruitment drive for eligible candidates aspiring to serve as Assistant Section Officers. The vacancies are specifically offered through the LDCE process, which is open to departmental candidates fulfilling the required conditions and guidelines.

Applicants interested in this opportunity should carefully review the advertisement and ensure they meet all eligibility criteria before applying. This notification provides detailed information about application procedures, eligibility, and important dates for candidates to participate in the recruitment examination successfully.
