IBPS Clerk Selection Process

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts a two-stage online examination process for candidates applying to the IBPS Clerk Recruitment. Applicants must successfully appear in both stages to be considered for final selection. Below are the details of the Preliminary and Main Examinations.

Preliminary Examination

The Preliminary Examination is an online objective test totaling 100 marks. The exam duration is 1 hour and consists of three sections. Candidates must achieve the cut-off marks in each section as determined by IBPS to qualify for the Main Examination.

Name of Tests Questions Marks Duration

Main Examination

Candidates who qualify in the Preliminary Examination will be shortlisted for the Main Examination. This stage is conducted online for 200 marks. The syllabus and structure of the Main Examination are outlined below.
