DME Assam Result 2025

The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Assam has officially released the shortlist of candidates for the Grade-III (Non-Technical) recruitment. The roll numbers of provisionally qualified candidates have been published based on their performance in the written test conducted on 30 March 2025.

It is further notified that the subsequent stages of the recruitment process include the Skill Test and Document Verification. The detailed schedule for these rounds has been issued along with the result. Shortlisted candidates must attend in person at the allotted venue on the mentioned dates. The complete roll number list can be accessed on the official DME Assam website.

How to check DME Result ?

The results of the DME Assam Grade-III (Non-Technical) written examination have been published on the official website of the Directorate of Medical Education, Assam. The result is available as a PDF document containing the roll numbers of candidates who have been provisionally shortlisted for the next stage of recruitment.

To verify your status, download the result PDF and use the search option (Ctrl+F) to locate your roll number. If your number is listed, you are eligible to appear for the Skill Test and Document Verification. Candidates must carefully check the allotted date, board, and group mentioned against their roll number for the verification process.

For candidate convenience, direct links to the official result notice and the complete list of shortlisted roll numbers are provided in the Important Links section below.

Schedule for Skill Test and Document Verification

All candidates whose roll numbers are listed in the published shortlist must appear for the Skill Test and Document Verification. The sessions will be conducted from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on the assigned dates.

Venue: Office Campus of the Directorate of Medical Education, Assam, Sixmile, Khanapara, Guwahati-781022.

Dates: 22, 23, and 24 September 2025, as per the roll number groups mentioned in the official notice.

Documents Required for Verification

Candidates are required to bring all original documents listed below for verification, along with one set of photocopies to be submitted at the venue.

DME Admit Card: Original admit card issued for the written examination.

Photo ID Card: A valid government-issued photo identification proof.

Date of Birth Proof: Birth Certificate or Class X pass certificate.

Educational Certificates: All pass certificates from HSLC onwards.

Caste Certificate: Applicable for candidates belonging to reserved categories, if relevant.

PwD Certificate: Applicable for Persons with Disabilities, if relevant.

Employment Exchange Registration Certificate: A valid registration card issued by the employment exchange.

Photographs: Two recent passport-size photographs.
