Eligibility Criteria for SSC Police Recruitment

Nationality/ Citizenship: Applicants must be citizens of India to be eligible for this recruitment process.

Age Limit: Candidates should be between 18 and 25 years as of 01.07.2025. Birth dates must fall between 02.07.2000 and 01.07.2007.

Age Relaxation: Relaxation in the upper age limit is applicable for reserved categories as per government rules.

SC/ ST candidates receive 5 years relaxation, OBC candidates 3 years, and Ex-Servicemen are also eligible for 3 years.

Educational Qualification: Applicants must have passed 10+2 (Senior Secondary) examination from a recognized board or institution.

Professional Qualification: Male candidates must hold a valid LMV (Motorcycle or Car) driving license at the time of PE&MT. Learner licenses will not be accepted.
