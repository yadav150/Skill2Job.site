Assam Police Result 2025

The State Level Police Recruitment Board (SLPRB), Assam, is set to release the final results for Sub Inspector and Fire Services vacancies by 10th October 2025. Candidates who qualify will be issued appointment letters for the advertised positions.

The appointment letters distribution is planned for the 25th and 26th of October 2025, with candidates expected to join by November 2025. The results will be available on the official SLPRB website, with relevant links provided for easy access.

Further, results for other Assam Police recruitments, including the Constable (AB/UB) post, are also expected in October 2025. However, these results are currently on hold pending judicial clearance from the Gauhati High Court due to ongoing petitions related to the recruitment.
