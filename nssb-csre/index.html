Selection Process

The recruitment involves three stages: written exam, computer/skill test, and interview for shortlisted candidates.

Computer Proficiency Test (CPT):

CPT is mandatory for all clerical and allied posts. Marks obtained do not contribute to the final score.

It evaluates basic computer skills for candidates shortlisted from the written exam. Minimum qualifying score is 30%.

Recruitment Tiers:

Tier I: General Paper (MCQ), 200 marks, 3 hours. Negative marking: 1/3rd per incorrect answer.

Tier II: Clerical & Allied: Qualifying CPT (max 50 marks). Uniform Services: Qualifying Physical Eligibility Test as per Annexure-I.

Tier III: Interview contributes 5% of the total marks from Tier I written examination.

Q1: What is the last date to apply for NSSB CSRE 2025?
