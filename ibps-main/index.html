IBPS PO Mains Admit Card 2025

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the admit cards for the Probationary Officer (PO) and Management Trainee (MT) positions under the Common Recruitment Process (CRP). Applications for CRP PO/MT-XV were accepted from 1st July to 21st July 2025. The admit cards for the online main examination were officially released on 1st October 2025, allowing candidates to access and print their hall tickets in preparation for the exam.

Selection Process of IBPS PO/MT

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts a two-stage online selection process for candidates applying to the IBPS PO/MT recruitment. Candidates are required to appear in both the Preliminary and Main examinations to progress in the selection process. Detailed information about each stage is provided below.

Preliminary Examination

The Preliminary Examination is an online objective test carrying a total of 100 marks. It has a duration of 1 hour and consists of three sections. Candidates must meet the cut-off marks in each section, as determined by IBPS, to qualify for the Main Examination. Selection for the Main Exam is based on performance in the Preliminary Exam.

English Language 30 30 20 Minutes
Quantitative Aptitude 35 35 20 Minutes
Reasoning Ability 35 35 20 Minutes
Total 100 100 60 Minutes

Main Examination

Candidates who qualify in the Preliminary Examination will be eligible to appear for the Main Examination, which carries a total of 225 marks. The Main Exam evaluates candidates across multiple sections, including reasoning, computer aptitude, general awareness, and English language. The detailed syllabus and structure of the Main Exam are outlined in the table below.
