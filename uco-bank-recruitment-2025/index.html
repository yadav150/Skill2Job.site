Eligibility Requirements

Age Limit: Candidates must be between 20 and 28 years as on 01.10.2025. This means the date of birth should fall between 02.10.1997 and 01.10.2005, both inclusive.

Age Relaxation: Category-wise relaxation is provided as follows:

Category Relaxation
SC/ST 5 years
OBC-NCL 3 years
PwBD 10 years
PwBD (OBC/NCL) 13 years
PwBD (SC/ST) 15 years

Local Language Proficiency: Applicants must be proficient in at least one local language of the state they are applying for. Language proficiency includes reading, writing, speaking, and understanding. A language test will be conducted during document verification, except for candidates presenting 10th or 12th mark sheets/certificates showing prior study of the local language.

Educational Qualification: Candidates should possess a graduate degree from a recognized university or institute approved by the Government of India or its regulatory bodies. Graduation must be completed on or after 01.04.2021 with mark sheets and provisional/final degree certificate.

Application Fee: The category-wise application fee is as follows:

Category Fee Amount

SC / ST NIL
PwBD Debit Card / Credit Card / Internet Banking
