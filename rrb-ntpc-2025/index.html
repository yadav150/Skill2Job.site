RRB NTPC Recruitment 2025

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced a short notification for 8,860 Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) vacancies. This recruitment aims to fill positions at both Graduate and Undergraduate levels across various Zonal Railways and Production Units under Indian Railways.

The online application process for Graduate-level posts begins on 21 October 2025, while applications for Undergraduate-level posts will start from 28 October 2025. Eligible candidates are encouraged to apply through the official RRB website.

RRB NTPC Selection Process

The RRB NTPC recruitment selection is conducted in several stages to ensure a fair evaluation. Initially, candidates must appear for the 1st Stage Computer Based Test (CBT).

Those who qualify in the first stage will be eligible for the 2nd Stage CBT. Depending on the post, candidates may then undergo a Typing Skill Test or a Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT).

The final stages of selection include thorough Document Verification followed by a Medical Examination to ensure candidates meet all eligibility criteria for the respective posts.
