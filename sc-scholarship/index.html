Merit-Based Scholarships for OBC and SC Students

5 October 2025

The Directorate of Welfare of SC & BC, Assam has published an official notification calling for applications under multiple Financial Incentive and Assistance programs. These initiatives are specifically designed to support students from the SC and OBC communities for the Academic Year 2025-26.

Students who meet the eligibility criteria and wish to apply can submit their applications online via the myScheme portal. All necessary details, guidelines, and instructions for the application process are provided on the portal.

Eligibility Requirements

Common Criteria for All Schemes: Applicants must have an active DigiLocker account to complete the application process. Additionally, the annual income of their parents or guardians should not exceed ‚Çπ5,00,000.

UPSC Mains Financial Assistance (SC & OBC): Candidates should have successfully cleared the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Preliminary Examination conducted in 2025.

Meritorious Student Incentives (SC & OBC): Applicants are required to have achieved at least 80% marks in the HSSLC examination held in 2025.

How to Check Whether You Are Eligible

Students can easily verify their eligibility for the schemes and see which programs suit them based on their academic performance and exam results. To do this, candidates need to visit the myScheme Eligibility Check Portal. Please click here to access the portal.

Once on the portal, follow the instructions on the first page for eligibility checking. Select your college or institute, provide the required details, complete the CAPTCHA, and submit the form. The portal will then display your eligibility status along with details of the schemes you can apply for.

Students belonging to SC and OBC communities who meet the eligibility criteria for the Academic Year 2025-26 can apply.

2. How can I submit my application?

Applications can be submitted online through the myScheme portal.

3. What is the required income limit for eligibility?
