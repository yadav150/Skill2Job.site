Employment Exchange Registration

Employment Exchange Registration in Assam allows citizens to officially enroll themselves for better access to government and private employment opportunities across the state.

Through this registration process, eligible candidates can ensure they are considered for upcoming jobs, training programs, and various career schemes directly supported by the government of Assam.

Important Update: Candidates who registered with Employment Exchanges in Assam before 2nd January 2024 must re-register via the Sewa Setu Portal by 31st December 2024. Failure to re-register will invalidate their Employment Exchange Registration. Click the link for more details.

Employement Exchange Registration Details

Name of Department: Directorate of Employment and Craftsmen Training, Assam manages the entire Employment Exchange Registration process in the state.

Eligibility: All Indian citizens aged 14 years and above who are permanent residents of Assam are eligible to register.

District: Applicants can register in only one Employment Exchange within their district of permanent residence in Assam.
