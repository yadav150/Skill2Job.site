Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit: Applicants should be between 18 and 40 years of age as of 1st January 2025. The upper age limit is relaxable by 2 years for Ex-Servicemen, 3 years for OBC/MOBC, 5 years for SC/ST, and 10 years for Persons with Disability (PwD).

Nationality & Domicile: Candidates must be Indian citizens as defined in Articles 5 to 8 of the Constitution and permanent residents of Assam. A valid Permanent Resident Certificate or Employment Exchange Registration Certificate must be submitted as proof of domicile.

Educational Qualification: Minimum qualification is Class VIII pass, and the maximum is HSSLC (Class 12) or equivalent. Candidates with higher qualifications than the maximum are ineligible. A self-declaration as per ‚ÄòAnnexure-I‚Äô is required.

APSC Grade IV Selection Process

The selection for APSC Grade IV posts will be conducted in two phases:

Phase-I (Screening Test): Candidates will appear for an OMR-based Multiple Choice Question (MCQ) test to shortlist applicants. The test carries 100 marks and lasts 2 hours. Questions will be set at Class VI-VIII level.
