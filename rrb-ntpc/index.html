Selection Process of RRB NTPC Vacancy

The selection for RRB NTPC posts involves multiple stages, including the 1st Stage Computer Based Test (CBT), 2nd Stage CBT, Typing Skill Test or Computer Based Aptitude Test (as applicable), followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination. Candidates are shortlisted strictly based on merit at each stage.

1st Stage Computer Based Test (CBT)

The 1st Stage CBT is a screening test. It consists of 100 objective-type questions to be completed in 90 minutes, with a total of 100 marks. Negative marking is applied at 1/3 mark for each wrong answer. The subjects include Mathematics, General Intelligence and Reasoning, and General Awareness. The questions are generally in conformity with the educational standards prescribed for the posts.

The normalized score of the 1st Stage CBT is used to shortlist candidates for the 2nd Stage CBT. Minimum eligibility percentages for various categories are UR-40%, EWS-40%, OBC (NCL)-30%, SC-30%, ST-25%, with a relaxation of 2 marks for PwBD candidates if required.

2nd Stage Computer Based Test (CBT)

Shortlisting for the 2nd Stage CBT is based on normalized marks from the 1st Stage CBT. The exam consists of 120 objective-type questions to be completed in 90 minutes, with a total of 120 marks and 1/3 negative marking for wrong answers. Subjects are the same as in the 1st Stage CBT.

Normalized marks from the 2nd Stage CBT are used to shortlist candidates either for Typing Skill Test/Computer Based Aptitude Test (for relevant posts) or directly for Document Verification for posts without these tests. Minimum eligibility percentages remain the same as the 1st Stage CBT.

Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT)

CBAT is conducted only for candidates applying for the Station Master post after qualifying the 2nd Stage CBT. Candidates must score a minimum T-Score of 42 in each test battery. Selection for CBAT is limited to eight times the number of vacancies for each community. Vision certificates must be submitted in the prescribed format, failing which candidates will not be allowed to appear.

Candidates must qualify in all CBAT test batteries. The test is available in English and Hindi only, and no negative marking is applied.

Typing Skill Test (TST)

TST is applicable for posts including Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Accounts Assistant cum Typist, and Account/Junior Clerk cum Typist. It is qualifying in nature, and marks are not added to merit. Candidates equal to eight times the number of vacancies are called for the test.

Candidates must type at least 30 WPM in English or 25 WPM in Hindi on a personal computer without editing tools or spell check. Hindi typing is available in Kruti Dev and Mangal fonts.

Document Verification (DV)

Candidates are called for Document Verification based on merit and the number of vacancies. Appointment is subject to passing a Medical Fitness Test, verification of educational and community certificates, and antecedents/character verification.

RRBs recommend the names of shortlisted candidates, but final appointment is offered only by the respective Railway Administrations.

FAQs ‚Äì RRB NTPC Recruitment 2025

Q1: What is the selection process for RRB NTPC 2025?

The selection process includes 1st Stage CBT, 2nd Stage CBT, Typing Skill Test/CBAT (if applicable), followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination. Shortlisting is strictly based on merit.

Q2: How many stages are there in the Computer Based Test (CBT)?

There are two stages: 1st Stage CBT for screening, and 2nd Stage CBT for final shortlisting. Subjects include Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, and General Awareness.

Q3: Who needs to appear for the Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT)?

Only candidates applying for the Station Master post must appear for CBAT. They must score a minimum T-Score of 42 in each battery to qualify for further selection.

Q4: What is the Typing Skill Test (TST) requirement?

TST is for posts like Senior Clerk cum Typist and Junior Accounts Assistant cum Typist. Candidates must type 30 WPM in English or 25 WPM in Hindi on a personal computer.

Q5: What documents are required for Document Verification?

Candidates must provide original educational certificates, community certificates, vision certificate (if applicable), and other required documents. Appointment is subject to passing the Medical Fitness Test and verification.
