Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) has published two official job notifications to recruit candidates for the positions of Graduate Engineer and Diploma Engineer. Interested and eligible applicants may apply.

Organization Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL)
Name of Post Graduate Engineer & Diploma Engineer
No. of Posts Not specified in the advertisement
Educational Qualification Graduation / Diploma
Application Mode Online
Application Fee As per category

IOCL Recruitment Details

1. Graduate Engineer
No. of Posts: Not Announced
Branch: Chemical, Electrical & Instrumentation
Age Limit: Applicants from the General/EWS category must not exceed 26 years of age as on 1st July, 2025.
Educational Qualification: Candidates must hold a B.Tech/BE or an equivalent degree with at least 65% marks for General/EWS/OBC-NCL and 55% marks for SC/ST/PwBD, obtained as a full-time regular course from AICTE/UGC-recognized Institutes/Universities in the relevant discipline.

2. Junior Engineer / Officer
No. of Posts: Not Announced
Branch: Chemical, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation
Age Limit: Applicants from the General/EWS category must not exceed 26 years of age as on 1st July, 2025.
Educational Qualification: Candidates should possess a three-year Diploma with at least 65% marks for General/EWS/OBC-NCL and 55% marks for SC/ST/PwBD, obtained as a full-time regular course from AICTE/UGC/State Board recognized Institutes/Universities in the respective disciplines.

IOCL Application Fee

Graduate Engineer: Applicants belonging to SC/ST/PwBD categories are fully exempted from paying the application fee. Candidates from other categories must pay a non-refundable fee of Rs. 500/- (Rupees Five Hundred) plus applicable GST.

Junior Engineer / Officer: Applicants belonging to SC/ST/PwBD categories are fully exempted from paying the application fee. Candidates from other categories are required to pay a non-refundable fee of Rs. 400/- (Rupees Four Hundred) plus applicable GST.

How To Apply for IOCL Recruitment 2025

Eligible and interested candidates can apply online for these vacancies by visiting the official IOCL Recruitment Portal. Applicants are advised to follow the steps mentioned below to complete the online application process.

Step 1: Go to the Important Web-Links section and click "Online Application Form".
Step 2: Select "New User? Register Here" and complete registration.
Step 3: Log in again through the "Online Application Form" link.
Step 4: Fill in personal and academic details and upload documents.
Step 5: Pay the fee (if applicable) and submit the form.
Step 6: Print the submitted application for reference.
