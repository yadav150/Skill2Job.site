ADRE Final Result 2025

The State Level Recruitment Commission (SLRC) successfully organized the written examination for 12,673 Grade-III and Grade-IV vacancies under the Assam Direct Recruitment (ADRE). These written tests were held in September and October 2024, conducted under the supervision of the newly formed Assam State School Education Board (ASSEB). In the previous ADRE 2023 drive, nearly 20 lakh applications were received across all categories. The written examination consisted of five papers, which were conducted in multiple shifts on different dates.

Following this, the Skill Test and Interview rounds for shortlisted candidates were conducted in 2025. On 7th March 2025, SLRC officially announced the provisional results for both Grade-III and Grade-IV posts. The results were made available on two official websites, where applicants could check their scores using their Application Number and Password. Additionally, SLRC released the paper-wise cut-off details of the Assam Direct Recruitment Exams.

As per the latest update from the Government of Assam, the much-awaited ADRE Final Results 2025 are expected to be declared on 10 October 2025 for Grade-III posts and on 15 October 2025 for Grade-IV posts.
