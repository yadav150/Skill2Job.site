ADRE Final Result 2025

The State Level Recruitment Commission (SLRC) successfully organized the written examination for 12,673 Grade-III and Grade-IV vacancies under the Assam Direct Recruitment (ADRE). These written tests were held in September and October 2024, conducted under the supervision of the newly formed Assam State School Education Board (ASSEB).
