NEC Scholarship 2025

The North Eastern Council (NEC) has issued the official notification for the NEC Scholarship for the academic year 2025-2026. This scholarship aims to support students of the North Eastern (NE) Region in pursuing higher education.

Students studying in various disciplines and levels at recognized institutions, either within the North-East or across other parts of the country, are eligible to apply online for this scholarship.

NEC Scholarship Details

Name of Scholarship: NEC Merit Scholarship, a prestigious scheme aimed at empowering deserving students from the North Eastern Region with financial assistance for higher studies.

Academic Year: 2025-2026 academic session, covering a wide range of courses, enabling students to continue their education with proper guidance, opportunities, and government-funded scholarship benefits.

Total No. of Scholarships: 1600 Nos., awarded annually to eligible applicants, ensuring equal opportunity and fair distribution of financial support among students of all recognized disciplines and levels.

Awarding Authority: North Eastern Council (NEC), under the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region, Government of India, Shillong, committed to uplifting education and promoting growth of the NE region.

Helpline Number: 0120 - 6619540, available for addressing queries, offering guidance to applicants, providing necessary clarifications, and supporting students during the scholarship application process and completion.

Email ID: helpdesk[at]nsp[dot]gov[dot]in, dedicated to assisting applicants with issues, receiving feedback, handling concerns, and ensuring smooth communication throughout the online scholarship submission process.

Eligibility Criteria for NEC Scholarship 2025

Students are required to meet the specified eligibility conditions in their previous qualifying examination in order to be considered for the NEC Scholarship.

Resident-ship: Only students who are permanent residents of any North Eastern State are eligible to apply for this scholarship scheme.

Duration: The scholarship will be granted for the full duration of the course, provided renewal conditions under Para 6 are met. No extension beyond the prescribed period is allowed.

Other Scholarship Holder: Students already receiving financial assistance or scholarships from other sources are not eligible. Sponsored or employed candidates will also not be considered under this scheme.

Year of Awarding Scholarship: The scholarship is granted only in the first year of the course. In subsequent years, only renewals will be entertained based on eligibility and rules.

Courses: Students admitted to Diploma, Degree, Postgraduate, M.Phil, or Ph.D programs in government-recognized institutions can apply. For M.Phil and Ph.D, eligibility begins after official university registration. Internships or training programs are excluded.

Educational Qualification for NEC Scholarship 2025

Diploma: Candidates must have passed HSLC, HSSLC, Secondary, or Senior Secondary examination from a recognized State or Central board with at least 60% aggregate marks.

Degree: Applicants should have successfully cleared HSSLC or Senior Secondary examination from a recognized State or Central board with a minimum of 60% marks in aggregate.

Postgraduate: Students must hold a Bachelor‚Äôs degree in any discipline (BA, BSc, B.Tech, BE, MBBS, LLB, BCA, BBA, etc.) from a recognized university or institute with a minimum of 60% marks overall.

M.Phil./Ph.D: Applicants must possess a postgraduate degree in any field from a recognized university with at least 60% marks in aggregate to be eligible.

Selection Process of NEC Scholarship 2025

The merit lists will be prepared from among applications that have been fully verified, following the prescribed guidelines and procedures of the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).

For M.Phil. and Ph.D. candidates, selection will be based on the research topic or synopsis, which must be directly relevant to the issues and development challenges of the North Eastern Region. Purely theoretical or basic research may not be given preference.

The scholarship is awarded solely on merit. Merit lists are determined exclusively by marks obtained in the qualifying examination, adhering to the level-of-course-wise allocation of slots for each state.

Q1: Who is eligible for the NEC Scholarship 2025?

Students who are permanent residents of any North Eastern State, meeting course-wise academic and income eligibility, can apply for this scholarship for Diploma, Degree, PG, M.Phil, or Ph.D.

Q2: What is the scholarship amount for NEC Scholarship 2025?

The annual scholarship amount ranges from Rs 20,000 for Diploma to Rs 30,000 for M.Phil./Ph.D courses, applicable for both fresh and renewal cases.

Q3: How can I apply for NEC Scholarship 2025?

Applications must be submitted online through the National Scholarship Portal (https://scholarships.gov.in). Register, fill the form, upload required documents, and submit before the last date.

Q4: What documents are required for NEC Scholarship application?

Mandatory documents include PRC/COI, income certificate, qualifying examination details, bank passbook, and for M.Phil./Ph.D., a synopsis signed by authorized guide or director.

Q5: How is the selection done for NEC Scholarship?

Selection is merit-based, using marks from qualifying exams. For M.Phil./Ph.D., research relevance to North Eastern Region issues is also considered during evaluation.
