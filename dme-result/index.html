DME Assam Final Result 2025

The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Assam will announce the final selection list for 2,473 vacancies under the Grade-III (Technical) and Grade-III (Non-Technical) categories on 12th October 2025. This list includes candidates who have successfully cleared the written examination, skill test, and document verification stages.

Applicants whose names appear on the final merit list will be officially appointed under DME, Assam. The distribution of appointment letters is expected to be conducted on 25th or 26th October 2025. Candidates can check their results directly from the official DME Assam website or through the important links section provided in this article.

Post Name: The recruitment covers positions under Grade-III (Technical) and Grade-III (Non-Technical) categories for eligible candidates.

Exam Date: The examination process was held in September 2025 across various examination centers in Assam.

Appointment: Distribution of appointment letters is scheduled for 25th or 26th October 2025.

Joining Date: Selected candidates are expected to join their respective posts in November 2025.

How to Check DME Assam Final Result

Candidates who appeared for the DME Assam Grade-III Skill Test and Document Verification can now check their final results by visiting the official website of the Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Assam. The results are published in a downloadable PDF file containing the roll numbers of all provisionally selected candidates.

To verify your selection status, download the official result PDF and use the Ctrl + F search feature to locate your roll number within the document. If your roll number appears in the list, you are shortlisted for final appointment under DME Assam.

The direct link to download the result PDF is available in the Important Weblinks section of this article, allowing candidates to access their results quickly and conveniently.
